It is sometimes said that a sentence expresses a complete thought.
This is a notional definition: it defines a term by the notion or idea it conveys.
The difficulty with this definition lies in fixing what is meant by a 'complete thought.' There are notices, for example, that seem to be complete in themselves but are not generally regarded as sentences: Exit, Danger, 50 mph speed limit...On the other hand, there are sentences that clearly consist of more than one thought.
